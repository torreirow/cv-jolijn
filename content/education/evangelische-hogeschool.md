## Foundation Year (Basisjaar)

**Institution**: Evangelische Hogeschool Amersfoort<br>
**Location**: Amersfoort, Netherlands<br>
**Period**: September 2023 - July 2024<br>
**Program Type**: Foundation Year

### Program Overview

Broad academic orientation year with emphasis on personal development, communication, and analytical thinking. This foundation year provided a comprehensive introduction to higher education and helped develop essential academic and personal skills.

### Key Focus Areas

- Personal development and self-reflection
- Academic writing and research skills
- Communication and presentation skills
- Analytical and critical thinking
- Study skills and time management
- Career orientation and exploration

### Skills Developed

- **Academic Skills**: Research, writing, critical analysis
- **Communication**: Verbal and written communication, presentation skills
- **Personal Development**: Self-awareness, goal-setting, reflection
- **Study Skills**: Time management, organization, independent learning

### Learning Outcomes

The foundation year provided:
- A solid academic foundation for further studies
- Clarity in educational and career choices
- Enhanced communication and interpersonal skills
- Improved analytical and critical thinking abilities
- Strong preparation for specialized higher education programs

### Significance

This foundation year was instrumental in:
- Transitioning from secondary to higher education
- Exploring different academic and career paths
- Developing essential skills for university-level studies
- Building confidence and academic maturity

### Connection to Current Studies

The skills and insights gained during this foundation year directly contributed to success in the International Public Policy and Leadership program, particularly in areas of communication, analytical thinking, and academic writing.
